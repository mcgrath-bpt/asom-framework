## Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
- Information that can be used to identify an individual. 

## Functional Safety Assessment (FSA)
- A formal process to evaluate the potential impact of a data processing activity on individuals' privacy rights and freedoms. It is required when a data processing activity is likely to result in a high risk to individuals' privacy rights and freedoms.

## Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
- A formal process to evaluate the potential impact of a data processing activity on individuals' privacy rights and freedoms. It is required when a data processing activity is likely to result in a high risk to individuals' privacy rights and freedoms.
